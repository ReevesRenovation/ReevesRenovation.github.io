WELCOME TO Reeves Renovations

About us

Reeves Renovations specialise in all types of general building work including extensions, loft conversions, home refurbishments and electrical services throughout Surrey and surrounding areas.

Reeves Renovations was founded in …... by John Reeves. Prior to starting Reeves Renovations, John spent many years working for himself gaining extensive knowledge of the building industry and forming strong relationships with other trusted members of the trade. Reeves Renovations has now evolved into a team of local skilled tradesmen who have all come together after working alongside one another for many years. We have a wealth of experience in all areas of general building services including extensions, loft conversions, full home refurbishments and the fitting of bathrooms and kitchens. We are NICEIC registered and can assist with the complete wiring of your home, or the testing, inspection and remodelling of existing electrics. The company has gone from strength to strength as we continue to take on new and challenging projects. All our work is undertaken by well-trained builders and fully insured.

KITCHENS

From dreams to reality

Whether you require a small part of your kitchen to be remodelled or a full refurbishment, Reeves Renovations can help with making your dream kitchen a reality.

All aspects undertaken

We specialise in carrying out knock through and side return kitchen extensions but can also simply assist with decorating, plastering, plumbing or electrical works to your kitchen.

Working together

Reeves Renovations have developed strong relationships with local high-end kitchen design companies. We are regularly introduced to customers with a view to undertaking the groundwork for, and installation of, beautiful bespoke kitchens.

We're here to help

If you know you want to develop your kitchen but aren't sure how to go about it, we'd be happy to discuss your needs further. Whether you plan to renovate or extend, we'll work with you to decide on everything up to and including the important finishing touches.
